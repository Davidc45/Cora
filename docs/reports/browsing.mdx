The Reports page is your window into what the community has flagged. All reports are publicly visible and sorted newest-first, so you can stay on top of recent activity without signing in.

## The reports feed

Every report appears as a card on the main Reports page. Each card shows:

- **Title** — the subject of the report
- **Description snippet** — a short preview of the full report body
- **Status badge** — the current moderation state of the report
- **Vote score** — the net total of upvotes minus downvotes
- **Comment count** — how many community comments the report has received

### Report statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Unconfirmed | Newly submitted; not yet reviewed by the community |
| Community Supported | The community has broadly upvoted the report |
| Disputed | The report has significant downvotes or community disagreement |

## Opening a report

Click any report card to open the detail page. The detail page shows the full report, including:

- The complete title and status badge
- The author's username
- The full description
- An attached photo, if one was uploaded
- Vote buttons (up and down arrows)
- The comments section

<Note>
  You need a signed-in account with a verified phone number to cast votes or post comments. Browsing is open to everyone without an account.
</Note>
